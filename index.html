Project Overview 🚀

ClipKar is an innovative web service that transforms your topics into rich video tutorials. Just enter a subject—like "Python Frameworks"—and ClipKar delivers a bespoke training session, complete with explanations and practical demos, powered by AI.

Key Features 🧩

  • 📝 Topic-to-Tutorial: Input any subject and get a curated video lesson.
  • 🤖 AI Generation: Combines scripts, voiceovers, and visuals automatically.
  • 🎯 Streamlined Learning: No more searching—just ask and watch.
  • 🗃️ History: All your past tutorials, one click away (MVP+).

Logo & Visual Identity 🎬

Concept: The play button suggests video; the lightbulb filament and spark (yellow rectangle) symbolize ideas and learning.
Colors: Tech blue, spark yellow, crisp white.
